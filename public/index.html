About
Twitter Feed Heatmap is a data visualization app with a node.js backend and vanilla js frontend. It uses the Twitter, Google Maps and IBM Watson APIs to stream live tweets around the globe and perform sentiment analysis using the contents of the tweets. Click on the icons to the left to find out more about me!
